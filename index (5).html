Common Questions

What is a Gynecologic Oncologist?

A specialist trained in the comprehensive surgical and medical treatment of cancers of the female reproductive system, including ovarian, uterine, cervical, vulvar, and vaginal cancers.

What are Minimally Invasive Surgeries?

These include Laparoscopic and Robotic surgeries performed through small incisions, designed for faster recovery, reduced scarring, and less post-operative pain compared to traditional open surgery.

What is HIPEC treatment?

Hyperthermic Intraperitoneal Chemotherapy (HIPEC) is a highly concentrated, heated chemotherapy treatment delivered directly to the abdomen during surgery, commonly used for advanced ovarian and peritoneal cancers.

Where does Dr. Sarma practice?

Dr. Sarma practices across Ahmedabad, Bengaluru, and Mumbai — offering consultations at leading tertiary care hospitals in these cities.
